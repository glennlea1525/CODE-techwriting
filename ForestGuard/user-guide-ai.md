# User Guide: ForestGuard v4.2

This was generated using Gemini AI

**Your Digital Sentinel for the Great Outdoors**

Welcome to **ForestGuard**. Whether you are a professional ranger or a dedicated conservationist, this guide will help you navigate the 2026 suite of forest monitoring tools. Let’s keep the canopy green and the data clean.

---

## 1. Quick Start: Getting Grounded

Before you head into the brush, ensure your device is synced.

1. **Launch & Sync:** Open the app while on Wi-Fi to download the latest **Local Satellite Overlays**.
2. **GPS Calibration:** Tap the **Compass Icon** in the bottom right to calibrate. Ensure high-accuracy mode is toggled **ON** in your system settings.
3. **Biometric Login:** ForestGuard uses secure Face/Touch ID to ensure that sensitive environmental data remains protected.

---

## 2. The Dashboard (The "Grove")

The main screen is your command center. It features a real-time heatmap of your assigned sector.

* **Green Pulse:** Baseline health; no anomalies detected.
* **Yellow Ripple:** Potential acoustic anomaly (e.g., unauthorized chainsaw noise or heavy machinery).
* **Red Spike:** Immediate thermal alert (potential fire) or confirmed illegal activity.

---

## 3. Reporting an Incident

If you spot an issue manually, follow the **"Capture-Tag-Send"** workflow:

1. **Capture:** Tap the **Large Plus (+)** button. The camera will automatically enable "Metadata Overlay," stamping the exact coordinates and elevation onto the photo.
2. **Tag:** Select the category: *Fire, Illegal Logging, Poaching Sign,* or *Species Sighting.*
3. **AI Description:** Use the **Voice-to-Text** feature to describe the scene. Our AI will automatically extract keywords to prioritize the report in the central queue.
4. **Send:** If you have a signal, hit **Upload**. If not, the report will be saved to your **Outbox** and sent automatically via the **Satellite Link** (if equipped) or when you return to base.

---

## 4. Offline Navigation & Safety

Forests aren't known for their 6G coverage. ForestGuard is designed to work entirely offline.

* **Breadcrumbs:** This feature is **ON** by default. It drops a digital marker every 50 meters, allowing you to backtrack even if you lose your sense of direction.
* **SOS Signal:** In an emergency, triple-tap the **Power Button** (or the in-app SOS toggle). This triggers a high-frequency emergency burst to overhead low-earth orbit (LEO) satellites.

---

## 5. Pro-Tips for Longevity

* **Dark Mode:** Keep this enabled to save up to **15% battery life** on OLED screens.
* **Cache Management:** Go to **Settings > Storage** once a week to clear "Old Map Tiles" and keep the app snappy.
* **External Sensors:** If you are using the *ForestGuard Acoustic Pods*, sync them via the **Peripherals** menu to see live audio feeds.

---

> **Note:** ForestGuard is a tool for monitoring and reporting. In the event of an active wildfire or dangerous confrontation, prioritize your physical safety over data collection.
